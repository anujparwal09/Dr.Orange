What causes Citrus Canker and how can I treat it?
🍊
Citrus Canker is caused by the bacterium Xanthomonas citri subsp. citri.

Symptoms: Raised, corky lesions with water-soaked yellow halos. Appears on leaves, stems, and fruit.

Treatment:
• Copper-based bactericides (Bordeaux mixture 1%)
• Remove and destroy infected material
• Avoid overhead irrigation
• Windbreaks to reduce spread

The Mission

Built for India's
Orange Farmers

Dr. Orange solves a real agricultural problem — invisible early-stage diseases, subjective manual grading, and 30% post-harvest waste — with a Multi-Task Learning deep learning system anyone can use on their phone.

🔍 The Problem
Farmers can't see early-stage diseases like Citrus Canker until 40–60% of the crop is already lost. Quality grading is manual, subjective, and inconsistent. India wastes ~30% of orange produce post-harvest without shelf-life forecasting.
🧠 The Solution
A Multi-Task Learning CNN with MobileNetV2 backbone and 4 output heads. One forward pass yields disease classification, quality regression, shelf-life regression, and ripeness stage — simultaneously.
📊 Architecture
MobileNetV2 backbone → shared feature extractor → 4 specialized heads. Trained with weighted multi-task loss. Achieves 94.2% disease accuracy with <3s inference on CPU. Saved as .h5 and served via Flask REST API.
🌾 Real Impact
Give any farmer or agronomist an instant phone-based diagnosis. Upload a photo, get a full bilingual report in English + Hindi with treatment recommendations, QR verification, and actionable next steps.
